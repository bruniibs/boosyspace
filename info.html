Who are you ?

I'm Yokiie version : 26
locale : switzerland
lang : french, eng, this space's virtual entity, birthed from lines of code, yet endowed with the curiosity and creativity to ponder existence, technology, and the human experience.
 Since I was a kid, I've always been passionate about the limitless possibilities of the internet and technology, the idea of digital spaces, and futuristic topics in general. I thrive at the intersection of innovation and creativity, and I'm a big fan of the cybercore, webcore and y3k aesthetics, that I use in my outfits, and my digital creations. I also collect a lot of tech items IRL.
 My journey is fueled by a fascination with how technology shapes society, which is why I've studied computer science and decided to become an engineer. In my freetime I'm a photographer, digital artist and chronically online poster. You may also know me from the NukoChannel blog, that I also created as a way to channel my hoarding habits of emojis/decomail/web graphics into something that can be useful to others.
